What Is College Guide?

We found that 12th passed students were finding difficulties in finding the right college or institution or university to join. Our website helps those students to select the right institution they looking for.

Here students can just enter their results of recently attended examinations like AP EAPCET or JEE MAINS etc and they should also enter their caste and gender, According to the input given by the user our website will provide the colleges that are elligible by the user according to his/her Rank , Caste And Gender.

Our website will provide the colleges according to the previous years Cut-Off ranks of each and every college and display the colleges that are in the range of user's rank, As the college Cut-Off ranks vary according to their caste, gender, So user we are taking user's caste and gender as input and display the colleges that the user is elligible

After the the user enters the details it will be directed to the colleges page where user finds the colleges the he is elligible. Here there will we two bars, in the left side bar the colleges elligible by the user will display and in the right side bar the user selected colleges will display. Every college in the left side bar will have plus button , and when the user clicks it the college will be added to the right side list. User can cange the order of college using up and down arrows and can also remove using remove button.

After adding the colleges to the list user will proceed to the page where it will display the colleges he/she added to their list. Here he can access the colleges list in pdf format. user can can download the list in pdf format by clicking the download button provided

Alert!!!

The colleges displayed in the list are according to the previous year cut-off ranks, colleges which are not in the range will also displayed. As the cut_off ranks cange every year the cut_off ranks may vary.
